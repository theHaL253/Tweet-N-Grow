Twitter is a platform where people share financial opinions. An influential tweet can affect a company's stock. Our team created a sentiment analysis flask app to provide users with an objective view of conversations. The app analyzes tweet sentiment and gives an overall "public opinion" of a company. This is useful for understanding the public's perception of a potential investment.

Developed by Bhuvan Biju, Chirag Singh, Long Do, and Michael Clark.
